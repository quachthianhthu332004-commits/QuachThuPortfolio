## Overview

**Project:** EarthTrek – Trekking Service Platform  
**Role:** Business Analyst  
**Team Size:** 3 members  
**Duration:** 4 months (Dec 2025 – Mar 2026)

EarthTrek is a centralized digital platform that supports users in planning trekking trips, accessing related services, and managing transactions within a unified system.


## Problem Statement

### Key Problems

- Trekking activities are fragmented across multiple channels  
- No centralized platform for trip planning and service management  
- Difficulty in tracking orders and service status  
- High entry barrier for new users due to lack of guidance  

### Business Drivers

- Growing demand for trekking but lack of specialized platforms  
- Shift from ownership → rental behavior  
- Need to reduce entry barriers for beginners  
- Increasing demand for safety and guided experiences  
- Opportunity to build a scalable service ecosystem  


## Solution Overview

A centralized platform integrating trip planning, service booking, and transaction management.

### Core Capabilities

- Plan and manage trekking journeys  
- Rent or purchase equipment  
- Book tour guides  
- Manage orders and payments in one system  

### Scope

**In Scope**

- Trip planning & itinerary  
- Equipment rental & purchase  
- Tour guide booking  
- Cart, order & payment  
- User account  

**Out of Scope**

- Hotel / restaurant booking  
- Weather prediction  
- Social features  


## Key Features

- Trip planning & itinerary creation  
- Equipment browsing & management  
- Tour guide booking  
- Cart & order lifecycle  
- Online payment integration  
- Order tracking & cancellation  


## System Design & Process

End-to-end workflows were designed to align business requirements with system behavior.

- User journey: planning → booking → payment → tracking  
- BPMN (current vs proposed)  
- Workflow design for cart, order & payment  

These artifacts ensured stakeholder alignment and supported development implementation.

## Requirements

### Functional Requirements

- Trip planning & location search  
- Product browsing & cart  
- Tour guide booking & payment  
- Order tracking & cancellation  
🔗 [Full](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1OQrgtjmHu6Ioj9hnnN1Fq0PxymL3TnNvh4kYjgbtmR8/edit?usp=sharing)

### Non-functional Requirements

- Search response ≤ 3s  
- Payment processing ≤ 5s  
- Availability ≥ 99.5%  
- Support up to 10,000 users  
🔗 [Full](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1OQrgtjmHu6Ioj9hnnN1Fq0PxymL3TnNvh4kYjgbtmR8/edit?usp=sharing)

### Business Rules

- Order cancellation only in valid statuses  
- Quantity limits per product  
- Dynamic price calculation  
- Payment validation before confirmation
